Who am I?

My name is Eva, a 23-year old from Cologne, a beautiful city in Western Germany. I am a very happy person; I am always smiling and laughing about something. I like to dance, go out for dinner and meet my wonderful friends as often as possible!

One of my favourite hobbies is traveling to countries I have never been before.
I love getting to know new cultures and habits to broaden my horizon.

Why this blog?

As already mentioned I am a girl that loves to travel. And I am aware that there are many other girls out there who would also love to explore new countries, but are afraid of traveling alone or are unsecure about the local conditions, because women don't have equal rights as men in some countries.

Because of that I want to show these girls the spots I have been to and encourage them to start their own adventure and to take over the world!
